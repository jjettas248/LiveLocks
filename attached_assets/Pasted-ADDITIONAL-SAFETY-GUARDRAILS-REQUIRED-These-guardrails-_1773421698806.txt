ADDITIONAL SAFETY GUARDRAILS — REQUIRED

These guardrails apply to the canonical market rebuild and must be enforced during implementation.

---

GUARDRAIL 1 — Game State Market Gating

Market availability must respect game state in addition to sportsbook line presence.

Rules:

Full-game markets:

* full_total and full_spread may be available pre-game and during live play.

First-half markets:

* h1_total and h1_spread may be available pre-game and during the first half.
* After halftime begins, these markets must automatically set available = false.

Second-half markets:

* h2_total and h2_spread must remain available = false until halftime has started.
* They may become available only when the engine has both:

  * a valid halftime game state
  * a sportsbook second-half line.

This rule prevents H2 markets from appearing pre-game or during the first half.

---

GUARDRAIL 2 — Probability Safety

modelProb must always be a valid probability value.

Rules:

* modelProb must be clamped to the interval [1, 99].
* If calibration or math produces NaN, Infinity, or undefined:

  * set available = false for that market
  * do not render the market.

The UI must never display probabilities outside the [1%, 99%] range.

---

GUARDRAIL 3 — Projection vs Book Line Separation

Engine projections must never be rendered as sportsbook lines.

Rules:

* projection is an internal model value.
* bookLine must always originate from a sportsbook feed.
* If no sportsbook line exists:

  * available = false
  * render the market as "Unavailable".

Do not render projected totals or spreads as if they were sportsbook lines.

---

GUARDRAIL 4 — Helper Function Deprecation Safety

resolveSpreadVerdict and resolveVerdictForMarket must not be deleted immediately.

Process:

1. Mark both helpers with a @deprecated comment.
2. Run a project-wide search to verify they are unused outside the NCAAB UI.
3. Only remove them if no other modules reference them.

This prevents breaking admin tools, alert pipelines, or diagnostic components.

---

GUARDRAIL 5 — Top Plays Performance Limit

The Top Plays feed must limit the number of rendered results.

Rules:

* Aggregate all available markets across all games.
* Sort by Math.abs(edge) descending.
* Render only the top 20 markets globally.

This prevents excessive DOM rendering during large slates.

---

GUARDRAIL 6 — Confidence Tier Generation

The engine must assign confidenceTier during market construction.

Example thresholds (engine-side):

edge ≥ 12 → ELITE
edge ≥ 8 → STRONG
edge ≥ 4 → VALUE
else → NONE

The UI must not recompute confidence tiers.

---

These guardrails must be implemented alongside the canonical market rebuild to ensure numerical stability, correct market visibility, and predictable UI behaviour.
